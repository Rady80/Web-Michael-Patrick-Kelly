❤️ Biographie Michael Patrick Kelly ❤️ Love

April 22, 2025 – The title TRACES of the new album by Michael Patrick Kelly carries symbolic power. He is no longer merely a seeker exploring themes of values and meaning in art; rather, he has made a lasting mark on European music history. Following several global hits and over 20 million records sold, his last album “B-O-A-T-S“ (2021) marked the most successful years of his solo career.

The album proved to be a true hit collection (five Top 5 radio hits, multiple international #1s, Gold and Platinum in several countries, over 250 million streams), followed by arena tours and headliner slots at major festivals in front of a total of 500,000 spectators. Now, Michael Patrick Kelly is taking the next step:
"The One" is the first single release from his sixth studio album.

"The One" resonates like a promise, reflecting the widespread longing for stability and commitment in a constantly changing world. Michael Patrick Kelly takes a different perspective on relationships than most love songs do.

Michael Patrick Kelly: "There are countless songs about the beginning and end of love. But very few celebrate the kind of love that endures through highs and lows and fights for each other. ‘The One’ is about a one-life stand. It’s a process, and—as the lyrics say—‘Forever in the making’.”

The classic MPK effect: you believe him when he sings about enduring love in this vibrant, inviting uptempo track. Encouraging and timely, he sings from a place of truth—the musician and peace ambassador who writes songs rooted in real stories.

A top-tier production team (including Michael Ilbert, Daniel Flamm, Johannes Herbst) wraps his unmistakable four-octave voice in an uplifting guitar pop-rock sound. MPK: “It’s important to me to make real ‘handmade music’ that comes from the heart and not from AI tools.”

The recordings for “The One” took place at the legendary AIR Studio in London, featuring Samuel Dixon, Dan McDougall, and Carlos Garcia. The music video was filmed in Deadvlei, Namibia—a ghost town with petrified trees, symbolizing the traces of time.

“The One” is available everywhere and follows his latest successful collaboration “Rebellion” with R3HAB and Shaggy.

About Michael Patrick Kelly: The Irish-American singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist was born into a famous family band. At 15, he wrote his first global hit, earning over 50 gold and platinum awards and performing in stadiums.

At 27, he retreated into a monastery for six years before returning with renewed energy and an even more powerful solo career. His hits like “Beautiful Madness”, “Blurry Eyes”, and “Wonders” are now internationally acclaimed.

On television, he captivates audiences across generations on shows like “The Voice of Germany” and “Sing meinen Song.”

For 25 years, Michael Patrick Kelly has supported global humanitarian and peace initiatives. In 2017, he created the PeaceBell from war debris—a moving bell of peace that opens each of his concerts.
